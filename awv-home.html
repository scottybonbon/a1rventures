What we build

We don't ship water. We ship the source.

01 — Technology

Atmospheric generation

Proprietary air-to-water systems with multi-stage filtration, UVC sanitation, and configurable mineralization — engineered for demanding environments.

02 — Product

A1R · still + sparkling

A premium beverage brand in aluminum and glass. Mineral-balanced, pH-neutral, made without groundwater extraction.

03 — Model

Scalable deployment

A modular fleet plus large-scale bottling — venues, campuses, resorts, industry, and government, with a second site planned for 2027.
