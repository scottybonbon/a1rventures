Investor FAQs

What will Air Water Ventures trade as?

Upon completion of the business combination, the company is expected to list on Nasdaq under the ticker AWVE. Until then, the security trades as Inflection Point Acquisition Corp. III.

How is the transaction structured?

A business combination (de-SPAC) with Inflection Point Acquisition Corp. III at a $200M pre-money equity value, with $96.0M of committed PIPE capital and a pro forma enterprise value of $339.5M (assuming 0% redemptions).

When is the transaction expected to close?

Closing is subject to shareholder and regulatory approvals and customary conditions. Timing will be set in the definitive proxy statement/prospectus filed with the SEC.

How much capital has been committed, and from whom?

$96.0M in PIPE capital, anchored by Inflection Point and Southern Glazer's, with $50.0M funded at announcement and $46.0M at close.

Where can I find official documents?

All filings are available free of charge via the SEC's EDGAR system at sec.gov, and are linked in the SEC Filings section above.

Who are the transaction advisors?

Cantor Fitzgerald & Co. is acting as placement agent for the PIPE in connection with the business combination.
