Air Water Ventures is the company behind A1R — atmospheric water systems, a premium beverage brand, and a deployment model built to put the source wherever water is needed.

[ MISSION + VISION ]

Water shouldn't depend on extraction.

Mission

Make water, on-site.

Convert atmospheric humidity into premium drinking water — produced where it's consumed, with no groundwater extraction, no impact on freshwater aquifers, and no single-use plastic required.

Vision

The next source of water.

Water demand is climbing as reliable supply becomes less certain. The next decade will be defined by resilience — and by water made closer to where it's consumed.

A projected 40% global freshwater supply shortfall by 2035 · Source: investor presentation, June 2026 (WEF-sourced).

[ THE STORY ]

From sky to shelf.

A1R was built in South Florida — proving the technology in one of the world's most demanding venues markets, then scaling it into bottling infrastructure.

Origin

The machine works.

Proprietary air-to-water systems — the Alpha Airflow platform — engineered to operate from 20–43°C and 25–100% humidity on any simple power source, with real-time water-quality data and remote diagnostics.

Hialeah, FL

The facility scales.

A South Florida bottling facility with 100,000+ liters per day of capacity — still and sparkling, in aluminum and glass. A second facility is planned for 2027.

Miami

The venues sign.

A1R becomes the Official Water of the Miami HEAT and Inter Miami CF — the first air-to-water partnerships in the NBA and MLS.

Distribution

The network arrives.

Southern Glazer's Wine & Spirits — North America's largest distributor of wine, spirits, and non-alcoholic beverages — takes its first-ever equity stake in a beverage supplier and signs a national distribution agreement.

2026

The company goes public.

Air Water Ventures announces a business combination with Inflection Point Acquisition Corp. III at a $200M pre-money equity value, with a $96.0M PIPE — and a listing path to Nasdaq.
