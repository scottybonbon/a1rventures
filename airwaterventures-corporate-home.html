[ WHAT WE BUILD ]

We don't ship water. We ship the source.

1_Technology

Atmospheric generation

Proprietary air-to-water systems engineered for demanding environments — converting humidity into high-quality drinking water with multi-stage filtration, UVC sanitation, and configurable mineralization.

2_Product

A1R — still + sparkling

A premium beverage brand in 16oz aluminum and 500ml / 1L glass. Mineral-balanced, pH neutral, and made without groundwater extraction or impact on freshwater aquifers.

3_Model

Scalable deployment

Modular fleet from 30 to 3,000 liters per day — venues, campuses, resorts, industry, and government — plus large-scale bottling — a South Florida facility producing today, with a second site planned for 2027.

[ THE PROCESS ]

1_Collect

Air is pulled from the atmosphere; HEPA filtration clears impurities before anything else happens.

2_Condense

Warm air meets cooling coils. Humidity becomes water — the only extraction is from the sky.

3_Purify

Multi-stage filtration removes contaminants; mineral and taste profiles are tuned to spec.

4_Serve

Clean, high-quality water delivered on-site — continuously recirculated for freshness and sanitation.

5_Replenish

Water is replaced as it's consumed. No waste. No freight. No single-use plastic required.
