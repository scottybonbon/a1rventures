Mission & vision

A new source for the world's water.

The world treats water as something to find, pump, and ship. We treat it as something to make, cleanly, anywhere, on demand.

Mission

Reinvent where water comes from

Our mission is to reinvent where water comes from, and raise the standard for what it can be: premium water captured from air, on-site, at scale, and without extraction.

Vision

Water that costs the planet nothing

A future where pure, sustainable water is the standard: clean, exceptional water that costs the planet nothing.

Our values

What we will not compromise.

01

Be responsible

Every decision traces back to a single question: does this protect the source? We will not compromise on where our water comes from, or what it costs the Earth.

02

Earn trust

Investors, partners, and consumers will hold us to the highest standard. We welcome that. Transparency is not a disclosure requirement; it is how we build the confidence that sustains long-term value.

03

Hold high standards

Responsible doesn't mean ordinary. We hold every product, every experience, and every touchpoint to the highest standard, because the case for conscious luxury only works if the luxury is real.

04

Think limitlessly

The atmosphere is everywhere. So is our ambition. We operate with the conviction that scarcity is a failure of imagination, and that the right source, scaled correctly, changes everything.

Technology

How we make water from air.

Pull air in. Condense it. Purify, mineralize, and serve: clean, high-quality water, monitored end to end. Proprietary systems engineered for the world's most demanding environments.

1 · Generation

Atmospheric generation

The Alpha Airflow platform pulls humidity from the air and condenses it, engineered for 20–43°C and 25–100% humidity on any simple power source.

2 · Purification

Purification & taste

Multi-stage filtration, UVC sanitation, and configurable mineralization: pH-neutral, mineral-balanced, tuned to spec.

3 · Intelligence

Intelligence & uptime

Real-time water-quality data and remote diagnostics keep every unit monitored, from a venue dispenser to facility-scale bottling.

Sustainability

Made without extraction.

The model removes the extraction, freight, and single-use waste built into traditional water.

From the air

Made on-site

Less freight
