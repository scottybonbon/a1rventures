Technology

How the source works.

Proprietary air-to-water systems engineered for demanding environments — turning humidity into high-quality drinking water, monitored end to end.

1 — Generation

Atmospheric generation

The Alpha Airflow platform pulls humidity from the air and condenses it — engineered for 20–43°C and 25–100% humidity on any simple power source.

2 — Purification

Purification & taste

Multi-stage filtration, UVC sanitation, and configurable mineralization — pH-neutral, mineral-balanced, tuned to spec.

3 — Intelligence

Intelligence & uptime

Real-time water-quality data and remote diagnostics keep every unit monitored — from a venue dispenser to facility-scale bottling.

Sustainability

Water that costs the planet nothing.

The model removes the extraction, freight, and single-use waste built into traditional water — by design.

No extraction

From the air

Condensed from atmospheric humidity — no groundwater pumping, no impact on aquifers or local reserves.

Made on-site

Less freight

Producing where water is consumed removes long-haul shipping and its emissions.

Built to last

Less waste

Continuous on-site supply cuts single-use plastic; aluminum and glass are built for circularity.
